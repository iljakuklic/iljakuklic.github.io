Hello, Internets!

Posted on 1 April 2015.

This place is going to be a storage space for my ideas and thoughts. Unorganized and messy. Sometimes looking more like it was written by a random bad pun generator rather than a human being. Containing a weird mixture of small hidden gems (I hope there will be some eventually) and a heap of toxic and otherwise dangerous or at least utterly useless material (pretty sure about this).

Welcome to Braindump Junkyard.
